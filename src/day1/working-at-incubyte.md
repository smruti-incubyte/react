Incubyte Company Summary
Document Purpose: Professional overview of Incubyte's work culture, policies, benefits, and values for submission purposes.
​

Company Philosophy: Incubyte focuses on hiring talented individuals, maximizing their potential, and giving them autonomy. They emphasize extreme programming, DevOps ownership, and continuous learning through personal upskilling and mentorship from founders.
​

Work Policies
Flexible Hours: Employees choose their schedules but must be available for company, client, or team needs. Accountability is task-based, not hour-based.
​

Leaves: Unlimited time off with advance notice for planning; trusts employees to be reasonable and honest.
​

Remote Work: Fully remote, pan-India hiring with workations encouraged if quality is maintained.
​

Core Values
Incubyte expects high standards in:

Honesty & Integrity: Be genuine and value candor.

Accountability & Ownership: Overcome obstacles and follow through.

Respect & Discipline: Professional behavior always.

Continuous Learning: Eager skill development and readiness for training.
​
